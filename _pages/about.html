About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in English Linguistics atSungkyunkwan University, specializing in computational linguistics with a primary focus on pragmatic inference. My research employs a range of Natural Language Processing (NLP) methodologies, including computational modeling and corpus-based analyses, to investigate how AI language models process language in comparison to human language processing, aiming to develop more human-like models with pragmatic competence. In addition to my core research, I actively engage in interdisciplinary studies, exploring the applications of AI language models in fields such as applied linguistics, second language acquisition, and the social sciences.
