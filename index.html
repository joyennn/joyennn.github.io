About Me

I am a PhD candidate in English Linguistics at Sungkyunkwan University, advised by Prof. Hanjung Lee, in the Language and Cognition Lab.

My research focuses on how AI language models process language in comparison to humans, with the dual goal of developing more human-like models and achieving a deeper understanding of human language use. From this perspective, I approach pragmatic phenomena through computational modeling and corpus-based analysis.

In addition, I actively engage in interdisciplinary research by applying natural language processing (NLP) techniques to other fields such as language learning and the social sciences.

Research Interest

  • Pragmatics
  • Computational linguistics
  • AI-assisted language learning
